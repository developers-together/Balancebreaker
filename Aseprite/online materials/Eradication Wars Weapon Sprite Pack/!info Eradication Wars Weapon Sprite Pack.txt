Hello and welcome!

This pack contains over 50 handmade pixel art sprites of various cool futuristic weapons.

Format: Opaque pixel art PNG with transparent background.

Can be used in any 2D sidescrolling video game project as a held weapon sprite or as a menu/HUD icon for the held weapon in any 2D or 3D game.

--

UPDATE v1.1

Expanded the tiny/micro weapons section with these additions:
- micro weapons are now offered as individual images.
- micro projectiles are now offered as individual images.
- micro muzzles are now offered as individual images.
- added sheets for all tiny sprites with transparent backgrounds.
- renamed tiny weapon sheets to better describe them.
